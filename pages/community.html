PPA & Zero Solar Program

Switching to solar energy is one of the best decisions you can make today. Going solar can help you:

Reduce or Even Eliminate Energy Bills

As a homeowner, you are probably used to a good portion of your monthly budget going to your energy bill. After all, you need electricity to be comfortable. But theres a way to reduce or even do away with your electricity costs by switching to solar power.

Take Advantage of Incentives

Going solar has become more affordable today than it has ever been thanks to incentives. For instance, you can take advantage of substantial credits on your state and federal taxes.

Increase Property Value

Buyers are willing to pay a premium for homes with solar energy systems. So if you have plans to sell your residential property in the future, installing solar energy panels today is a great idea. Zillow reports up to a 5.1% increase in total home value!
